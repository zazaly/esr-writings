## []{#script-kiddie}Master Foo and the Script Kiddie {#master-foo-and-the-script-kiddie .title style="clear: both"}

</div>

</div>
:::::

A stranger from the land of Woot came to Master Foo as he was eating the morning meal with his students.

["[I hear y00 are very l33t,]{.quote}"]{.quote} he said. ["[Pl33z teach m3 all y00 know.]{.quote}"]{.quote}

Master Foo\'s students looked at each other, confused by the stranger\'s barbarous language. Master Foo just smiled and replied: ["[You wish to learn the Way of Unix?]{.quote}"]{.quote}

["[I want to b3 a wizard hax0r,]{.quote}"]{.quote} the stranger replied, ["[and 0wn ever3one\'s b0xen.]{.quote}"]{.quote}

["[I do not teach that Way,]{.quote}"]{.quote} replied Master Foo.

The stranger grew agitated. ["[D00d, y00 r nothing but a p0ser,]{.quote}"]{.quote} he said. ["[If y00 n00 anything, y00 wud t33ch m3.]{.quote}"]{.quote}

["[There is a path,]{.quote}"]{.quote} said Master Foo, ["[that might bring you to wisdom.]{.quote}"]{.quote} The master scribbled an IP address on a piece of paper. ["[Cracking this box should pose you little difficulty, as its guardians are incompetent. Return and tell me what you find.]{.quote}"]{.quote}

The stranger bowed and left. Master Foo finished his meal.

Days passed, then months. The stranger was forgotten.

Years later, the stranger from the land of Woot returned.

["[Damn you!]{.quote}"]{.quote} he said, ["[I cracked that box, and it was easy like you said. But I got busted by the FBI and thrown in jail.]{.quote}"]{.quote}

["[Good,]{.quote}"]{.quote} said Master Foo. ["[You are ready for the next lesson.]{.quote}"]{.quote} He scribbled an IP address on another piece of paper and handed it to the stranger.

["[Are you [*crazy*]{.emphasis}?]{.quote}"]{.quote} the stranger yelled. ["[After what I\'ve been through, I\'m never going to break into a computer again!]{.quote}"]{.quote}

Master Foo smiled. ["[Here,]{.quote}"]{.quote} he said, ["[is the beginning of wisdom.]{.quote}"]{.quote}

On hearing this, the stranger was enlightened.
