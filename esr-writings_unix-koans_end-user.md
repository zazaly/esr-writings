## []{#end-user}Master Foo and the End User {#master-foo-and-the-end-user .title style="clear: both"}

</div>

</div>
:::::

On another occasion when Master Foo gave public instruction, an end user, having heard tales of the Master\'s wisdom, came to him for guidance.

He bowed three times to Master Foo. ["[I wish to learn the Great Way of Unix,]{.quote}"]{.quote} he said ["[but the command line confuses me.]{.quote}"]{.quote}

Some of the onlooking neophytes began to mock the end user, calling him ["[clueless]{.quote}"]{.quote} and saying that the Way of Unix is only for those of discipline and intelligence.

The Master held up a hand for silence, and called the most obstreperous of the neophytes who had mocked forward, to where he and the end user sat.

["[Tell me,]{.quote}"]{.quote} he asked the neophyte, ["[of the code you have written and the works of design you have uttered.]{.quote}"]{.quote}

The neophyte began to stammer out a reply, but fell silent.

Master Foo turned to the end-user. ["[Tell me,]{.quote}"]{.quote} he inquired, ["[why do you seek the Way?]{.quote}"]{.quote}

["[I am discontent with the software I see around me,]{.quote}"]{.quote} the end user replied. ["[It neither performs reliably nor pleases the eye and hand. Having heard that the Unix way, though difficult, is superior, I seek to cast aside all snares and delusions.]{.quote}"]{.quote}

["[And what do you do in the world,]{.quote}"]{.quote} asked Master Foo, ["[that you must strive with software?]{.quote}"]{.quote}

["[I am a builder,]{.quote}"]{.quote} the end user replied, ["[Many of the houses of this town were made under my chop.]{.quote}"]{.quote}

Master Foo turned back to the neophyte. ["[The housecat may mock the tiger,]{.quote}"]{.quote} said the master, ["[but doing so will not make his purr into a roar.]{.quote}"]{.quote}

Upon hearing this, the neophyte was enlightened.
